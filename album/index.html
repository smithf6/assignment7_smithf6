About the Artist

Robert Morris was a sculptor who participated in artistic Minimalism. He focused on simplicity, form and function. Some of his notable works include the, "Untitled (Labyrinth)" "Untitled (Scatterpiece)" and "Untitled (L-Beams)". His work has been described as not having a single style, and using abstract ways to have the viewer interact with the art.

The Untitled Steamworks at Western Washington can be seen in different phases and is never quite the same as you left it.

Interaction with the art is a cornerstone of Robert Morris's work. Students and people visiting campus will often move the stones around and make stacks of rocks resembling cairns.
